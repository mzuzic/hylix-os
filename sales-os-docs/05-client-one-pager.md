## A day with your AI OS

- **7:00 — Morning brief on your phone.** Today's jobs and calls, who to
  follow up, cash position, overdue invoices. Read it from the truck.
- **9:30 — Before the sales call.** A one-page brief: who they are, history,
  what to say, what they'll object to, what to aim for.
- **11:15 — Quote from the job site.** Dictate the job into your phone.
  Your rate card is applied, the quote is drafted and formatted —
  **minutes, not days**. First quote in usually wins the job.
- **2:00 — After the meeting.** Follow-up email and proposal drafted from
  what was actually said. CRM updated. Nothing falls through the cracks.
- **5:00 — Money looks after itself.** Won job → invoice with payment link,
  drafted. Overdue invoices → polite reminders, ready to approve.

## How it works — the architecture

Five layers, each doing one job:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  YOUR TEAM — phone, laptop, voice                   │
│  briefs · quotes · dashboard · approvals            │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  CLAUDE (your accounts) + HYLIX PLUGIN              │
│  skills that know when to act: /quote, briefs,      │
│  follow-ups, call scoring — installed in one click  │
├──────────────────────────┬──────────────────────────┤
│  YOUR TOOLS (direct)     │  HYLIX AI OS SERVER      │
│  HubSpot · Gmail ·       │  Second Brain (memory)   │
│  Calendar · QuickBooks · │  SOPs (playbooks)        │
│  transcription           │  routines & agents       │
└──────────────────────────┴──────────────────────────┘
```

**1. Connectors.** Claude links directly to your tools using the open MCP
standard, with your own logins. Your CRM and email data flows straight between
your tools and your AI — it never passes through our servers.

**2. The Second Brain.** Your business's private memory, hosted per client
with isolated, token-secured access. Organized the same way for every module:

```
company/            who you are: tone, values, goals
<module>/profile/   stable config (rate card, payment terms, ICP)
<module>/state/     live items (deals, jobs, invoices)
<module>/events/    raw inputs (call transcripts, reviews, receipts)
<module>/library/   distilled lessons — what works, learned over time
```

**3. SOPs — your playbooks, centrally tuned.** How to build a quote, answer an
objection, chase an invoice. They live on our server, so when we improve one,
your system improves the same day — no updates to install. A weekly *distill*
routine turns raw events into library lessons: this is what makes the system
compound.

**4. Skills & plugin.** A one-click plugin installs lightweight skills into
your team's Claude accounts. Skills are triggers, not brains — "user described
a job → fetch the quote playbook and rate card, follow it." They never go
stale because the playbook is always fetched fresh.

**5. Automation, in three trust levels.** Level 1: your team asks, AI does,
human approves (day one). Level 2: scheduled routines — morning brief, weekly
review digest, payment reminders, drafted and waiting for approval. Level 3:
autonomous agents for routine actions you choose to delegate (e.g. replying to
5-star reviews) — added only when you're ready, running on our infrastructure.

**Walk-through — the 11:15 quote:** you dictate the job → the quote skill
triggers → fetches the current quote playbook + your rate card and tone from
the Second Brain → drafts line-item pricing → asks about anything critical
that's missing → you approve → it's saved to the deal and ready to send as an
invoice with a payment link. Elapsed: about two minutes.

**Works beyond Claude.** The server speaks MCP — the standard also supported
by ChatGPT and Gemini — so the core system meets you where you are, though the
full experience (skills, mobile agent, scheduled tasks) is best on Claude.
